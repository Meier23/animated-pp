# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 7, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Home Assistant - Unofficial App (the "App") handles information when distributed on Google Play and other app stores.

## 1. Who We Are

Developer: Home Assistant - Unofficial App Developer  
Contact email: ha-mgstools@outlook.com

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to the Android and Windows versions of the App, including versions distributed through Google Play.

This same policy may also be used for Microsoft Store submission for the Windows version of the App.

## 3. Data We Process

The App is designed to work primarily with data you provide directly.

Data categories processed by the App:

- Home Assistant connection data you enter:
  - Home Assistant server URL
  - Home Assistant long-lived access token
- App configuration and preference data:
  - Profile names and active profile selection
  - Accent color and UI preferences
  - Settings button position and minimized state
- Temporary technical state needed to render and operate the in-app dashboard WebView

We do not operate a developer backend that receives or stores your Home Assistant token as part of normal App operation.

## 4. How Data Is Collected

- Directly from you when you configure the App
- Locally on your device to persist settings required for App functionality
- From network responses returned by the Home Assistant server you choose

## 5. Why We Process Data

We process data only to provide the App's core functions:

- Authenticate and connect to your Home Assistant instance
- Load and display your Home Assistant dashboard in the App
- Save local preferences and profile settings
- Support profile switching and connection testing

## 6. Storage and Security

- Sensitive credentials (for example, Home Assistant tokens) are stored locally using platform secure storage APIs.
- Non-sensitive preferences are stored locally in app preferences.
- We use reasonable safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure.
- For stronger transport security, use HTTPS for Home Assistant connections whenever possible.

## 7. Data Sharing, Sale, and Advertising

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not share personal data for advertising or profiling.
- The App does not include third-party advertising SDKs.
- The App does not include behavioral analytics SDKs by default.
- Network requests are sent directly to the Home Assistant endpoint configured by you, and to external links only when you choose to open them.

## 8. Permissions and Device Access (Android)

The Android app requests Internet access to:

- Reach your configured Home Assistant server
- Load web content in the embedded dashboard WebView
- Open optional external links you choose

The App does not request sensitive runtime permissions such as Contacts, SMS, Call Logs, microphone, camera, or location for core operation.

## 9. WebView and Third-Party Content

The dashboard is rendered from your Home Assistant server using WebView.

- Content shown in the dashboard is controlled by your Home Assistant instance and its integrations.
- Cookies or browser-like storage used by that dashboard are handled by the platform WebView.
- External services connected through Home Assistant are governed by those services' own terms and privacy practices.

## 10. Device and Network Abuse Statement

In line with Google Play policies on privacy, deception, and device abuse, the App is not designed to:

- Perform hidden or unauthorized background activity
- Create botnet, proxy, or abusive traffic behavior
- Install, modify, or execute code from unknown sources outside normal platform mechanisms
- Interfere with, damage, or gain unauthorized access to devices, networks, or other apps

## 11. Transparency and Non-Deceptive Behavior

- The App's primary purpose is to provide a client interface for your own Home Assistant instance.
- The App does not claim affiliation with or endorsement by Home Assistant unless explicitly stated by trademark owners.
- We aim to present clear, accurate information about features, limitations, and data handling.

## 12. Data Retention and Deletion

- Data is retained locally on your device until you modify or remove it.
- You can delete local App data by clearing profile/settings data in the App or uninstalling the App.
- We do not keep a central remote copy of your configuration data in normal operation.

## 13. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 14. International Data Transfers

The App itself does not require a developer-operated central cloud backend for core use. Network traffic depends on the Home Assistant and third-party services you configure, which may process data in different jurisdictions.

## 15. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data (for example, access, correction, deletion, or restriction). Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can usually exercise control directly in the App settings or by uninstalling.

For privacy requests, contact: ha-mgstools@outlook.com
